Our products can be custom designed to fit each customers needs.

EACH™ S8

Applicable to vertical wells and inclined wells.
It provides the highest impact strength and middle abrasion resistance. High resistance to unidirectional impact. Micro fracture model. Fast rate of penetration, long lifetime and good repairability.


EACH™ T9

Applicable to vertical wells and inclined wells.
It offers higher impact strength and high abrasion resistance. High resistance to unidirectional impact. Micro fracture model. Faster rate of penetration, long lifetime and good repairability.


EACH™ T HOR

Used for horizontal wells.
It exhibits the highest abrasion resistance and high impact strength. Micro fracture model. Due to the large particle size of diamonds, it can withstand the high radial and axial impact force of drill bit.


EACH™ N Series

It provides high abrasion resistance and high impact strength at the same time, for using larger particle size diamond. It can accept intermittent impact energy. Its flat and special-shaped products are suitable for complex formation or hard formation.
