PDC for Oil & Gas Industry

Zhengzhou EACH Diamond Ltd. specializes in the production of oil and gas PDC cutters. Over the years, we have perfectly been combining overseas advanced technology with China's super abrasives’ production practice, and committing to designing, developing and manufacturing excellent products in line with international standards, to serve the increasingly challenging energy drilling industry. Our high-quality products can still maintain the excellent performance of long lasting, sharpness and efficiency under extreme underground drilling conditions.

We use ultra-high pressure technology to obtain higher diamond density and lowest metal content. Using high cleanliness of diamond micron powders and diamond recrystallization growth environment, we significantly improve the proportion of bond to bond between diamonds. The polycrystalline diamond composite produced is not a complete crystal, more similar to a complete crystal.

EACH T8 series

It is applicable to vertical wells and inclined wells.
It has the highest impact strength and high wear ratio. High resistance to unidirectional impact. Micro fracture model. It provides fast rate of penetration, long lifetime and good repairability.


EACH™ T HOR series

It is used for horizontal wells.
It exhibits the highest wear ratio and high impact strength. Micro fracture model. Due to the large particle size of diamonds, it can withstand the high radial and axial impact force of drill bit.


EACH™ N Series

A kind of high toughness PDC cutters.
It provides high wear ratio and high impact strength at the same time, for using larger particle size diamond. Micro fracture model. It can accept intermittent impact energy. Its flat and special-shaped products are suitable for complex formation or hard formation.
