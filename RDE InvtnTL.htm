R&D Team

Our R&D staff constitutes experts from Zhengzhou Research Institute in Abrasives and Grinding field. An engineering executive from White Dove Abrasives Group and a PhD in material science founded Each Diamond Ltd. Our R&D efforts are also supported by partner companies in oil and gas services.

Members of our R&D team have made contributions to technical development of China’s superabrasive industry. Our team inherits tradition of dedication and with their experience, we will keep on innovating.
