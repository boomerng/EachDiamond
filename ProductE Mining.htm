PDC cutters for Mining Industry

Each Diamond manufactures high performance PDC cutters for variety of drill bits such as core drill bits, no core drill bits and reamer drill bits. We have products that specialize for solid mineral drilling, coal mine drilling, hydrogeology drilling, water well drilling, engineering geology drilling, oil & gas drilling, civil engineering drilling, geothermal drilling and more.

Each Diamond uses the high-purity diamond micron powder and the best tungsten carbide, follows advanced manufacture techniques to manufacture PDC cutters. They offer tough abrasion resistance and impact strength. Our technological breakthroughs and continual improvement are widely recognized by our partners and customers.
