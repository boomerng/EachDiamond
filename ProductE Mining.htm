PDC Cutters for Mining Industry

Each Diamond manufactures the high performance PDC Cutters that are used for making a variety of drill bits, such as core drilling bits, no core drilling bits and reaming drill bits to break down hole rock in the applications of solid mineral drilling, coal mine drilling, hydro-geological drilling, water well drilling, engineering geological drilling, oil and gas drilling, civil engineering drilling, geothermal drilling and so on.

Each Diamond PDC Cutters are made of high-grade and high-purity diamond micron powders and the best tungsten carbide by the advanced manufacture technology. They offer high wear resistance and significant impact toughness. The technological breakthroughs and continual improvement are widely recognized by customers.
