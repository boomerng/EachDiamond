R & D

Performance of PDC Cutter
Hardness (diamond microhardness 7000-10000 kg/mm2), impact strength (Joule), fracture toughness (MPa).

The Performance and Wear

  1. Hardness: The higher the hardness of the diamond layer, the stronger its ability to resist abrasive wear, and then the higher of wear resistance.
  2. Impact strength: The higher the impact strength of PDC cutter, the less likely it is to be chipping wear.
  3. Fracture toughness: The higher the fracture toughness of the diamond layer, the less likely it is to be tiny chipping wear.

The Relationship between Hardness, Impact Strength, and Fracture Toughness
Hardness is negatively correlated with impact strength and fracture toughness. Impact strength is positively correlated with fracture toughness.

The Performance and Life

  1. When the impact strength of drilling is low, the higher the hardness, the longer the working life.
  2. When the impact strength of drilling is high, it is easy to cause chipping wear. The higher the impact strength and fracture toughness, the longer the working life.

The Performance and Rate of Penetration
Under the conditions of enough impact strength and fracture toughness, the higher the hardness, the higher the rate of penetration.

Testing of The Performance

  1. Hardness: Testing of the wear ratio.
  2. Impact strength: Testing of Heavy hammer impact.
  3. Fracture toughness: Testing of bending strength of diamond layer.

The Influence of Diamond Crystal Size on Performance

  1. Fine size, higher hardness. diamond crystals deform less, resulting in a smaller reduction in the strength of diamond crystal.
  2. Fine size, lower impact strength and fracture toughness. The diamond recrystallization is larger, and the strength of diamond recrystallization is lower than that of diamond crystals.
  3. Coarse size, lower hardness. Diamond crystals deform more, resulting in a greater reduction in the strength of diamond crystal.
  4. Coarse size, higher impact strength and fracture toughness. The diamond recrystallization is smaller.

The Influence of the Strength of Diamond Crystals on Performance

  1. Within a certain size range, the higher the original strength of diamond crystals, the higher the hardness.
  2. The finer the size, the smaller the deformation, and the less the influence.
  3. The coarser the size, the greater the deformation, and the less the influence.
