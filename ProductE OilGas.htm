We sinter surface treated diamond micron powders in a six dimensional presser by applying the high temperature and higher pressure (HTHP) process. Moreover, we rigorously follow our strict QA process to ensure products stability and reliability.

EACH™ T7 PDC cutters

Thermal Stability Product
T7 PDC cutters have an enhanced thermal stability on the basis of incomparable abrasion and toughness. Consisted of pure-white diamonds and the least micro interstitials between the diamonds, T7 exhibits a higher resistant to heat damage and diamond graphitization. Thermal abrasion makes PDC cutters over wear and failure prematurely. First, heat on diamond table expands the catalytic metals between diamonds, and then a thermal expansion stress ensued destructs micro structure of the diamond table, because the metals’ expansion coefficient is about 10 times that of diamond. Second, as graphite-to-diamond reaction is reversible, diamond may turn back to graphite due to the metals’ catalysis at high temperature and pressure on cutter edge.

Since thermal abrasion is often caused by deep drilling, or by lateral drill string vibrations in sidetracking and horizontal drillings, T7 PDC cutters with exceptional thermal stability are recommended for such applications.



EACH™ E7 PDC cutters

High Abrasive Product
E7 PDC cutters provide the advantages of excellent abrasion and thermal stability without sacrificing toughness. E7 features higher diamond concentration (HDC) that equates to a low metal content, and more perfect re-crystallized bonding diamond to diamond in the diamond table.

When PDC bits drill wells at same field site, a bit used mere high abrasion PDC cutters and another bit used E7 PDC cutters of high abrasion and thermal stability. The two bits keep the same rate of penetration (ROP) at the start. After PDC cutters are slightly worn, the former bit’s ROP begin to decline obviously, whereas E7 cutters continue to keep sharp and maintain its initial high efficiency, so considerably reducing cost per feet (CPF), and also reducing number of times of PDC cutters’ repair. E7 PDC cutters are welcomed in the vertical, directional and horizontal drilling applications for complicated and inter-bedded formations.



EACH™ E5 PDC cutters

High Impact Product
E5 PDC cutters offer high impact and adequate abrasion. The combination keeps long working life and high ROP for PDC drill bits, suitable for bit repairs.
