PDC for Oil & Gas Industry

Each Diamond Ltd. focuses on manufacturing PDC cutters for oil and gas drilling. With state of the art technology and years of expertise, we never stop improving PDC cutters. We sinter surface treated diamond micron powders in a six dimensional presser by applying the high temperature and higher pressure (HTHP) process. Moreover, we rigorously follow our strict QA process to ensure products stability and reliability.

EACH™ N Series

Most Tough PDC Cutters
Thanks to the perfect diamond recrystallization bonding diamond to diamond, N PDC cutters become a almost integrity crystal for the diamond table. While working, this structure exhibits the smallest chips from diamond cutting edge than any other polycrystalline diamond compacts. They contribute the most toughness and ideal sharpness, so provide long working lifetime and consistent rate of penetration. N PDC cutters are welcomed in the application of vertical, directional and horizontal well drillings in complicated and inter-bedded formations.



EACH™ T7 Series

Thermal Stability Product
Using extremely high pressure, T7 PDC cutters reach a super high diamond concentration, a lowest metal content. They prevent metals between diamonds from expanding and diamonds from be graphitized under such harsh working conditions, significantly avoiding products’ failure. They offer excellent thermal stability, high abrasion and moderate toughness. T7 PDC cutters are widely used in the application of oil and gas well drillings.
