COMPANY PROFILE

EACH DIAMOND LTD. focuses on manufacturing Polycrystalline Diamond Compact（PDC）cutters, and we continously seeking new ways to improve our PDC cutters. Our cutters exhibit high penetration rate, long-lasting, and cost-effective, and they are best applied in down-hole drilling. We are located in Zhengzhou High-tech Zone, a National Innovation Superabrasive Industry Park.

With outstanding knowledge and 30 years of professional experience, EACH DIAMOND team dedicates tremendous effort to the design, development and production of our products. Not only we ensure our products meet the international standards, but also satisfy the needs of our customers individually. Based on our personal initiative and professional integrity, we provide excellent service in energy, resources, and transportation industries, and become one of the most recognized innovators in the super abrasive industry. Our core values include innovation, value creation, collaboration and accountability.

EACH DIAMOND has a number of proprietary technologies, and the most advanced production lines. We also specialized in application test techniques. The rigorous quality management system guarantees the suitability, reliability and stability of our products. With excellent customer services, EACH DIAMOND has gained recognition and reputation in the global market. We greatly appreciate all of our customers’ trust and support.
