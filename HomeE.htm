INTRODUCTION

EACH DIAMOND LTD. focuses on manufacturing PDC cutters that reach the advanced level in China and meet international standards.

With outstanding knowledge and professional experience for 30 years, we dedicate ourselves to design, development and production. We have perfectly been worked the combination of the international advanced technology with Chinese super-abrasives' production practice.

We have a number of patents and proprietary technologies. The rigorous quality management system guarantees our products suitability, reliability and stability. We have gained recognition and reputation in the worldwide market.

EACHDIAMOND uses ultra HTHP technology, to make PDC cutters with higher density of diamond. Using a special treatment for diamond materials, and creating optimal growth environment for diamond recrystallization, we significantly improve the proportion of bond to bond between diamonds. Our high-quality products maintain excellent performance of long lasting, sharpness and efficiency under extreme underground drilling conditions.
